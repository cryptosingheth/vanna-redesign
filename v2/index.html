HF = book value ÷ debt = L / (L−1) — 10× target lands at HF 1.11, just inside the 1.10 line the guard defends.

01

One number governs the whole book.

Health factor = total collateral value ÷ total debt. Borrowing opens above 1.25; liquidation only triggers near 1.10 — the gap between them is a designed-in safety runway, not a hard cliff.

02

Priced by a manipulation-resistant oracle.

Positions are marked on a ~25-minute TWAP (Reflector) and the account is fail-closed — no fresh price, no risky action. You can't move the book by flickering a price.

03

Credit can move, but never leave.

Borrowed funds deploy to approved venues yet can never be withdrawn to a wallet — they stay inside the protocol as a marked claim the engine can always price and recall.
