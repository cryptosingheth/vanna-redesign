Unified portfolio margin

Isolated margin walls you in. Unified sets you free.

Isolated margin traps leverage inside one market. A unified portfolio margin account is cross-margined: every collateral, every borrowed asset, every strategy across every venue is marked to one oracle and offset into a single solvency ratio. A loss on one leg is absorbed by a gain on another before it ever touches your collateral.

The cage

Isolated margin

  • Every position is walled off in its own account.
  • The same borrowed asset can’t be reused across markets.
  • No delta-neutral, no multi-leg — one market at a time.
  • You babysit each position’s liquidation, one by one.
  • A gain in one market is stranded — it can’t offset a loss in another.
Narrow, retail experience
The desk

Unified portfolio margin

  • One account across spot, perps, options, prediction and yield.
  • One collateral line, reused across every market — headroom for up to 10×.
  • Delta-neutral and multi-leg strategies in one place.
  • One health factor for the whole book.
  • A loss in one market is offset by a gain in another.
